Welcome to Social Network for Music

Social Network for Music (SNM) is the first social network designed for music enthusiasts like you. Whether you're a music lover, a playlist curator, or an artist, SNM has something for everyone.

What We Offer

At SNM, you can create and manage your own music playlists, discover new tracks and artists, and connect with fellow music enthusiasts. Our platform provides a seamless experience for music sharing and discovery.

Key Features

  • Create and customize your playlists with your favorite songs.
  • Explore a vast library of music from various genres.
  • Connect with friends and share your musical discoveries.
  • Discover new tracks and artists based on your musical preferences.

Using Spotify APIs

SNM utilizes Spotify's APIs to provide access to a wide range of music tracks and information. You can learn more about Spotify's APIs here.

About this project

Our platform uses Node.js and Express.js to create a robust and scalable backend that can handle complex operations efficiently. We've developed custom API endpoints to facilitate seamless communication between the frontend and backend systems.
You can explore our API documentation here to gain a deeper understanding of our endpoints and data structures.
